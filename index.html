Frequently Asked Questions

What is AuraPlay?

AuraPlay is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of award-winning TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries and more – on thousands of internet-connected devices.


You can watch as much as you want, whenever you want, without a single ad – all for one low monthly price.

How much does AuraPlay cost?

Watch AuraPlay on your smartphone, tablet, Smart TV, laptop, or streaming device, all for one fixed monthly fee. Plans range from ₹149 to ₹649 a month. No extra costs, no contracts.

Where can I watch?

Watch anywhere, anytime. Sign in with your AuraPlay account to watch instantly on the web at auraplay.com from your personal computer or on any internet-connected device that offers the AuraPlay app.

How do I cancel?

AuraPlay is flexible. There are no annoying contracts and no commitments. You can easily cancel your account online in two clicks. There are no cancellation fees – start or stop your account anytime.

What can I watch on AuraPlay?

AuraPlay has an extensive library of feature films, documentaries, TV shows, anime, award-winning AuraPlay originals, and more. Watch as much as you want, anytime you want.

Is AuraPlay good for kids?

The AuraPlay Kids experience is included in your membership to give parents control while kids enjoy family-friendly TV shows and films in their own space.
